About Me

I’m a graphic designer based in Peru, focused on branding, social media, and graphics/posters. I’m particularly interested in building clear, functional visual systems with strong composition, hierarchy, and consistency across formats.

Most of my work consists of academic and conceptual projects, developed to explore ideas, strengthen my design thinking, and refine my technical skills while building a solid foundation in design.

I’m open to creative collaborations, commissions, and freelance opportunities.
